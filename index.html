GrowSocial: A Community Enterprise

Donate or Invest

Now is the opportunity for you to invest in Growsocial's vision and success!

You set up a profile for your business or personal identifying your role in the local economy (CSA, Beekeeper, Food Artisan, Forager, Teacher, Volunteer, etc).
You find people like you who are part of the local economy and you also find resources, markets, events and forums all based in your local area.
You can easily set up business site, whether you are a full time business or just someone who might occasionally have something to sell- like honey or seasonal fruits.
You can learn from and contribute to your “Localpedia” – a place where you can find information on local plants, conditions, and contribute to growing your community knowledge.
You can come together as a local community and work on initiatives that make your community better and find ways to act collectively such as forming tool banks, time banks.
GrowSocial is a global site with a local experience. We promote the site so that your potential customers, collaborators and beneficiaries can find you easily.

HOW YOU CAN BE A PART OF THIS?
